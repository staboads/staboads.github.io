What services do you offer?

We offer a range of services including talent scouting, contract negotiation, and career management.

How can I apply to be represented by your agency?

You can apply by submitting your resume and portfolio through our online application form on our website.

What is the process for talent scouting?

Our talent scouts regularly attend industry events and accept submissions through our website. Promising candidates are invited for an interview.

Do you charge upfront fees?

No, we do not charge any upfront fees. Our commission is based on the earnings we help you generate.

How do you select the talent you represent?

We look for individuals who show exceptional talent, dedication, and potential for growth. Our selection process includes reviewing submissions, interviews, and evaluations by our talent scouts.

What types of talent do you represent?

We represent a wide range of talent including actors, musicians, models, writers, and influencers.

Can you help with international opportunities?

Yes, we have connections and partnerships with agencies and companies worldwide to help our talent secure international opportunities.

What kind of support can I expect from your agency?

Our support includes career management, contract negotiations, marketing and branding, and professional development.

How do you handle conflicts of interest?

We maintain strict policies to ensure that there are no conflicts of interest. Our goal is to provide unbiased and dedicated support to all our clients.

What is your commission structure?

Our commission structure is competitive and varies depending on the services provided and the agreements made with our clients. We do not charge any upfront fees.

How often will I get updates from my agent?

Communication frequency varies based on the client's needs and activities. However, we ensure regular updates and are always available for our clients.

What should I bring to the initial meeting?

Please bring your resume, portfolio, headshots, and any relevant materials that showcase your talent and experience.

How do I terminate my contract with your agency?

Contract termination details are outlined in your agreement with us. We aim to handle all terminations professionally and amicably.
